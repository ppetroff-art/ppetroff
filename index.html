Hi, I'm Plamen — a concept artist and art director with a passion for building immersive worlds and compelling characters. I bring ideas from rough sketches through to polished visual concepts that guide entire productions.

I've worked across film, games, and animation — collaborating with directors and creative teams to develop the visual language of their stories. Whether designing a single hero character or establishing the entire look of an environment, I focus on the details that make a world feel real and lived-in.
